Payroll Management

Business owners have to juggle multiple responsibilities simultaneously. Outsourcing payroll is one way they can lighten their loads. FWC can streamline payroll operations by handling some or all of a small company’s payroll services and related tax obligations. Employers often entrust this critical business function to service providers that help them meet filing deadlines and deposit requirements. It’s important for business owners to understand, however, that unless they hire us that assume responsibility for those obligations, they may still be responsible for paying income tax and employee contributions to Social Security and Medicare and for paying additional Medicare taxes.
